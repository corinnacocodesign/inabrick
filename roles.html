How it works

Every child has an important role to play

Our structured brick-based sessions use clear roles to help children practise communication, listening, teamwork, problem solving and confidence in a fun, supportive setting.

🧱

The Engineer

Finds and selects the correct bricks, helping develop attention, organisation and listening skills.

📦

The Supplier

Delivers the correct pieces to the builder, encouraging communication, turn taking and teamwork.

🏗️

The Builder

Follows the instructions and builds the model, supporting patience, confidence and problem solving.

Why roles matter

Learning happens through doing

Each role gives children a clear responsibility within the group. This helps them understand how to work with others, listen carefully, communicate clearly and feel proud of their contribution.

Skills children practise

  • Teamwork and shared decision making
  • Clear communication and active listening
  • Turn taking and patience
  • Following instructions
  • Creative thinking and problem solving
  • Confidence and self-expression
