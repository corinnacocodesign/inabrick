The three brick-club roles

🟢 The Engineer

Gives instructions and describes what needs to happen next. This role builds expressive language, sequencing and leadership.

🔴 The Supplier

Finds the pieces, checks colours and sizes, and supports the team by listening carefully.

🔵 The Builder

Builds the model using the instructions and pieces provided by the team.

Roles make teamwork visible.

Children rotate roles so everyone gets a chance to lead, listen, organise and create.
