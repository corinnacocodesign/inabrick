Build & Breathe

Sound therapy, mindfulness and gentle play

Using singing bowls, chimes, rainsticks, drums and other calming sounds, Build & Breathe offers children a peaceful space to relax, regulate emotions, reduce anxiety and build resilience through mindful listening and play.
